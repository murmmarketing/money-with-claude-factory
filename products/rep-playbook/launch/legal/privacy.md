## 3. Data we collect

**a) Purchase and entitlement data:**
- Your **email address**, used to deliver the product, key your entitlement, and let you re-access your download/reader on any device.
- Order details: amount paid (including your chosen pay-what-you-want amount), currency, status, timestamp, and identifiers from the payment processor (e.g. a Stripe Checkout session ID or Gumroad sale ID).
- We do **not** store full payment card details. Card processing is handled by **Stripe** (see https://stripe.com/privacy) or **Gumroad** (see https://gumroad.com/privacy).

**b) Access / authentication data:**
- One-time magic-link tokens (short-lived) sent to your email to grant access.
- A signed, HttpOnly access cookie containing your email and an expiry, used to keep you signed in to the online reader and to verify your entitlement.

**c) Free-content / lead data:**
- Your **email address** and its source (e.g. "free chapter", "waitlist") when you request free content or join the waitlist, so we can deliver it and, if you opted in, send follow-up emails.

**d) Communications:**
- Emails you send us, and — if you opt in — your marketing/newsletter subscription status.

**e) Technical and usage data:**
- Basic analytics events (e.g. which page or article was viewed) and standard server/log data such as IP address, browser type, and timestamps, used for security, debugging, and improving the Service. We keep this minimal.

We do **not** intentionally collect special-category data. Please don't send us sensitive personal information.

## 4. How we use your data

We use personal data to:
- deliver the product you bought and let you re-access it (performance of our contract with you);
- authenticate you via magic link and secure your access (contract / legitimate interest);
- process and manage your payment and any refund via Stripe/Gumroad (contract);
- deliver free content you requested and send transactional emails such as your magic link and receipt (contract / legitimate interest);
- send marketing or product-update emails **only where you have opted in**, with an unsubscribe link in every message (consent);
- maintain, debug, secure, and improve the Service and prevent abuse and content piracy (legitimate interest); and
- comply with legal obligations such as tax and accounting records (legal obligation).

## 5. Legal bases (EU/UK users)

We rely on: **contract** (delivering and supporting the product), **consent** (marketing/newsletter emails; you can withdraw anytime), **legitimate interests** (security, anti-piracy, analytics, service improvement, transactional communication), and **legal obligation** (tax/accounting records). Where we rely on legitimate interests, we balance them against your rights.

## 8. Cookies and local storage

- **Essential access cookie:** a signed, HttpOnly cookie set only after you click your magic link, used to keep you signed in to the online reader and verify your entitlement. This is strictly necessary.
- **Local storage:** may be used to remember lightweight preferences (e.g. your reading position). This stays on your device.
- We keep analytics lightweight and avoid third-party advertising cookies. If we introduce any non-essential cookies, we will request consent where required.

You can clear cookies and local storage in your browser at any time; doing so will sign you out of the reader (you can get back in with a fresh magic link).

## 9. Data retention

- **Order and entitlement records:** kept for as long as needed to give you ongoing access to your purchase and to meet legal obligations.
- **Billing records:** retained as required by tax/accounting law (typically up to 7 years).
- **Lead / waitlist emails:** kept until you unsubscribe or ask us to delete them, subject to legal record-keeping.
- **Magic-link tokens:** short-lived and expired/deleted after use.
- **Logs / analytics:** kept for a limited period for security and debugging.

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to: access your data; correct it; delete it; restrict or object to processing; data portability; and withdraw consent (for marketing) at any time. EU/UK users may lodge a complaint with their supervisory authority. California residents have rights under the CCPA/CPRA, including to know, delete, correct, and opt out of "sale"/"sharing" (we do not sell or share personal data as defined).

To exercise any right, email **privacy@therepplaybook.com**. We will respond within the timeframe required by applicable law. We may need to verify your identity (e.g. via your purchase email). Note that deleting your account data may remove your ability to re-download the product.
