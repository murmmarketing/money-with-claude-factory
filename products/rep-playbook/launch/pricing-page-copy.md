## FAQ

**Is it a subscription?**
No. The Rep Playbook is a **one-time purchase**. You pay once and get the PDF, the cheat-sheets, lifetime access to the online reader, and free future updates. (That's deliberately different from HaulHQ, which is a monthly tool.)

**How do I get it after buying?**
Instantly. After checkout you'll get an email with a **magic link** to your download + online reader. No password to remember — click the link on any device to access it. Lost the email? Enter your purchase email on the site and we'll send a fresh link.

**What's pay-what-you-want?**
At checkout you choose your price, from a **$9 floor**. Most people pay $19. Pay more if it saved you money; pay the floor if that's where you're at. Either way you get the complete Playbook — there's no "cheaper, worse" version.

**Is the customs info accurate?**
It's current for **2026** and covers US, UK, EU, Canada, and Australia, including the 2025 US de-minimis change. Customs rules move, so figures are framed as informed estimates — and because you get free updates, your copy stays current. Always verify against your country's official guidance before a big order.

**Do I need to know anything already?**
No — it starts at "what's a W2C?" and takes you to placing and receiving your first haul confidently. If you're intermediate, the QC, sizing, and customs references are worth keeping regardless.

**Can I get a refund?**
Yes. If it's not what you expected, email within **7 days** of purchase and we'll refund it, no argument.

**Is this legal / are you selling reps?**
No. The Playbook is an **educational guide only**. We don't sell, source, or ship any products, and we're not affiliated with any agent or brand. What you choose to buy and import is your responsibility — the guide just makes sure you understand the process.

**Do you take PayPal / crypto?**
Payment is by card through **Stripe**. If a Gumroad option is live, that adds PayPal at checkout.
