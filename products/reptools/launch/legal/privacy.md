## 2. Summary (the short version)

- The **free tools work in your browser** and don't require an account. Your hauls are saved in your browser's local storage, not sent to us, unless you sign in and choose to save to the cloud.
- We collect your **email address** when you sign in for Pro (passwordless one-time code) and when you join a waitlist.
- We use **Stripe** to process payments — we never see or store your full card number.
- If you go Pro, we store the **hauls, items, notes, and photos** you choose to save.
- We don't sell your personal data.

## 3. Data we collect

**a) Data you don't have to give us (free tools):**
Free calculators and the Haul Builder Lite run client-side. The hauls you build there are stored in your browser's local storage on your device. We do not receive that data unless you sign in and sync.

**b) Account / authentication data:**
- Your **email address**, used to send one-time login codes and to key your account and entitlements.
- One-time login codes (stored hashed and short-lived) to verify sign-in.
- A signed session cookie (HttpOnly) containing your email and an expiry, used to keep you signed in and to check Pro access.

**c) Subscription and billing data:**
- Subscription plan, status, and billing period, and identifiers from Stripe (e.g. Stripe customer ID and subscription ID).
- We do **not** store full payment card details. Card processing is handled by **Stripe**; see their privacy policy at https://stripe.com/privacy.

**d) User Content (Pro):**
- Hauls and haul items you save to the cloud: titles, product URLs, prices, quantities, weights, agent and shipping selections, notes, and any **QC photos** you upload (stored in Supabase Storage).
- Public haul pages you choose to publish and their content.
- Price-watch entries (product links, labels, target prices).

**e) Communications:**
- Emails you send us and, if you opt in, marketing/newsletter subscription status.

**f) Technical and usage data:**
- Basic analytics events (e.g. which tool was used) and standard server/log data such as IP address, browser type, and timestamps, used for security, debugging, and improving the Service. We keep this minimal.

We do **not** intentionally collect special-category data. Please don't upload sensitive personal information in notes or photos.

## 4. How we use your data

We use personal data to:
- provide the tools and save/sync your hauls (performance of our contract with you);
- authenticate you and secure your account (legitimate interest / contract);
- process and manage your subscription and refunds via Stripe (contract);
- send transactional emails such as login codes, receipts, and price-watch reminders (contract / legitimate interest);
- send marketing or product-update emails **only where you have opted in**, with an unsubscribe link in every message (consent);
- maintain, debug, secure, and improve the Service and prevent abuse (legitimate interest); and
- comply with legal obligations (legal obligation).

## 5. Legal bases (EU/UK users)

We rely on: **contract** (providing the Service and Pro), **consent** (marketing emails; you can withdraw anytime), **legitimate interests** (security, analytics, service improvement, transactional communication), and **legal obligation** (tax/accounting records). Where we rely on legitimate interests, we balance them against your rights.

## 6. Who we share data with (processors)

We share data only with service providers ("processors") who help us run the Service, under agreements that require them to protect it:

- **Vercel** — website and application hosting.
- **Supabase** — database and file storage for accounts, hauls, and QC photos.
- **Stripe** — payment processing and subscription management.
- **Resend** — sending transactional and (opt-in) marketing emails, including login codes.

We may also disclose data if required by law, to protect our rights, or in connection with a business transfer. We do **not sell** your personal data.

By using the Service you understand your data may be processed in these locations.

## 8. Cookies and local storage

- **Essential cookie:** an HttpOnly session cookie for signed-in Pro users to keep you logged in and verify access. This is strictly necessary and set only after you sign in.
- **Local storage:** used to save your in-progress hauls on your own device for the free tools. This stays on your device.
- We keep analytics lightweight and avoid third-party advertising cookies. If we introduce any non-essential cookies, we will request consent where required.

You can clear local storage and cookies in your browser at any time; doing so will remove locally saved hauls and sign you out.

## 9. Data retention

- **Local haul data:** stays on your device until you clear it.
- **Account and cloud hauls:** kept while your account is active. If you delete a haul it is removed; if you close your account we delete or anonymize your personal data within a reasonable period, except records we must keep by law.
- **Billing records:** retained as required by tax/accounting law (typically up to 7 years).
- **Login codes:** short-lived and deleted/expired after use.

## 10. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to: access your data; correct it; delete it; restrict or object to processing; data portability; and withdraw consent (for marketing) at any time. EU/UK users may lodge a complaint with their supervisory authority. California residents have rights under the CCPA/CPRA, including to know, delete, correct, and opt out of "sale"/"sharing" (we do not sell or share personal data as defined).

To exercise any right, email **privacy@haulhq.app**. We will respond within the timeframe required by applicable law. We may need to verify your identity (e.g. via your account email).

## 11. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including HttpOnly session cookies, hashed one-time codes, restricted database access (service-role writes), and reliance on reputable processors. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 12. Children
