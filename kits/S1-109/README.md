# S1-109 — Branded Email Signatures as a Service

**Positioning:** Companies with 5–200 staff have ugly, inconsistent email signatures that
quietly leak professionalism on every email they send. You sell one clean, on-brand,
Outlook/Gmail/Apple-Mail-safe signature *system* — and charge per seat to roll it out.

**Exact target customer:** Ops/office managers and founders at 5–50-person B2B services
firms (agencies, law/accounting, real estate, clinics, SaaS). They already care about brand
and have a credit card. Find them via LinkedIn ("Office Manager" + city) and local business groups.

**The offer:** "One polished signature design + installable HTML for your whole team, done in 3 days.
$15/seat (min $150), or $12/seat for 20+."

**Price:** $10–30/seat, or flat team fees $150–600. Add a $49/seat/yr "keep it updated" retainer.

## Launch in 60 minutes
1. Open `signature-generator.html` in a browser — it's a working generator. Fill in name/title/
   phone/logo URL → it renders a bulletproof table-based signature and copies the HTML.
2. Make ONE gorgeous sample using a fake but realistic company (see `sample-signature.html`).
3. Screenshot it in Gmail + Outlook (paste the HTML into a compose window).
4. Put the sample + price on a one-pager (use `landing-copy.md`) — a Notion page or Carrd works.
5. Send the 3 outreach messages in `marketing.md` to 10 local firms.
6. **[YOUR MOVE]** Connect a way to get paid + agree pricing — see `YOUR-MOVE.md`.

## Why this is a real business, not a gig
Signatures change constantly (new hires, title changes, campaign banners). The install is
per-seat and annoying, so teams happily pay you to own it. Land 10 companies × 20 seats ×
$15 = $3,000 once, plus retainers. Delivery is copy-paste from the generator — near-zero marginal cost.
