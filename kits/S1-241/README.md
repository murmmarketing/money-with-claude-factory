# S1-241 — Prompt Pack & Template Vault (a real, sellable digital product)

**Positioning:** A curated, niche-specific vault of battle-tested prompts sold on a one-page
storefront. Not "1000 ChatGPT prompts" spam — a tight, opinionated kit for ONE profession that
saves them hours a week. This kit ships the **Freelance Web Designer & Developer Vault** — 20
prompts for the recurring, annoying parts of client work (proposals, scoping, bug triage, copy,
handoff docs). It's wired to Marius's murmweb.dev audience, but the storefront reskins for any niche.

**Target customer:** Freelance web devs/designers and small agencies who use Claude/ChatGPT daily
but write mediocre one-off prompts. They'll pay $29 to stop reinventing the proposal prompt every
week. Find them in r/freelance, r/webdev, r/web_design, Indie Hackers, and dev Discords.

**Offer:** "The Freelance Web Dev Vault — 20 copy-paste prompts for the boring parts of client work:
proposals, scope creep defense, bug triage, client-ready copy, handoff docs. $29. Free 5-prompt
teaser to try before you buy."

**Price:** $29 launch price (regular $39). Upsell a **$79 Pro Vault** (adds SOP docs, email
templates, a Notion client-portal template). The free 5-prompt teaser builds your email list.

## This is a REAL product — what's in the box
- `index.html` — a **working, deployable one-page storefront** (open it in a browser right now).
  Styled, responsive, with the pitch, the prompt-list preview, FAQ, and a checkout button you
  point at Gumroad/Stripe.
- `vault/freelance-web-dev-vault.md` — the **actual paid product**: 20 real, usable, structured
  prompts with fill-in variables and usage notes. This is what the buyer downloads.
- `vault/free-teaser-5-prompts.md` — the **free lead magnet**: 5 genuinely useful prompts to give
  away for an email address.
- `landing-copy.md` — headline/subhead/benefits/FAQ/CTA (also embedded in index.html).
- `marketing.md` — 3 ready-to-post launch pieces with exact places to post.
- `YOUR-MOVE.md` — connect Gumroad/Stripe, host the file, deploy the page.

## Launch in 60 minutes
1. Open `index.html` in your browser — this is your storefront, done.
2. Skim `vault/freelance-web-dev-vault.md` — the product is written and ready to sell.
3. Upload `vault/freelance-web-dev-vault.md` as a Gumroad/Stripe digital product ($29). Upload the
   teaser as a free email-gated download.
4. Put your checkout URL into `index.html` (one line — search `REPLACE_CHECKOUT_URL`).
5. Deploy `index.html` (Vercel/Netlify drag-drop, or a `/vault` route on murmweb.dev).
6. **[YOUR MOVE]** post the 3 launch pieces (`marketing.md`) and give the free teaser away. See
   `YOUR-MOVE.md`.

## Why this is durable revenue
Zero marginal cost per sale, no fulfillment, no calls. Reskin the same storefront + vault format
for the next niche (real-estate agents, therapists, Etsy sellers) and you've got a product line.
