# Worked Example — "FocusFlow" (Pomodoro/focus timer) · before → after

Use this as your **portfolio piece**. When a prospect asks "what do I actually get?", send them
this. It shows the full transformation and the reasoning, which is what earns the $200–800.

---

## BEFORE (typical indie listing — leaking installs)

- **Title:** `FocusFlow`
- **Subtitle:** `The best focus app`
- **Keyword field:** `focus, best, app, timer, good, productivity, focus app`
- **Description opener:** *"FocusFlow is an app I built to help you focus. It has a timer and
  some other features. I hope you like it! Please leave a review."*

**Diagnosis — the 3 ranking leaks (this is the free-audit deliverable):**
1. Title uses zero search volume beyond the brand — burning the single highest-weight field.
2. Subtitle wastes 18 chars on "The best" (nobody searches "best") and repeats "focus".
3. Keyword field repeats "focus"/"app" (already in title) and uses "best"/"good" (no search demand)
   — ~40% of the 100-char budget wasted.

---

## AFTER (optimized)

**Keyword targets chosen:** primary `pomodoro timer` · secondary `study timer`, `focus`,
`concentration` · long tail `adhd, deep work, habit, session`.

- **Title (25/30):** `FocusFlow: Pomodoro Timer`
- **Subtitle (28/30):** `Study focus & habit tracker`
- **Keyword field (96/100):**
  `study,concentration,deep,work,adhd,timer,productivity,habit,routine,break,session,timeboxing,exam`
- **Promotional text (163/170):**
  `New: full-screen focus mode + weekly stats. Beat procrastination one 25-minute sprint at a time —
  no account, no ads, no distractions.`

**Description (Apple — convert only):**
```
Turn scattered days into deep, focused work — 25 minutes at a time.

FocusFlow is a Pomodoro timer for students, remote workers, and anyone with ADHD who
wants to actually finish things. No account. No ads. No noise.

★ 4.8 · loved by 12,000+ focused people

• Beat procrastination with 25-minute focus sprints and timed breaks
• Build a streak — see your focused hours add up every week
• Full-screen focus mode that blocks the urge to app-switch
• Custom session lengths for deep work, study, or quick tasks
• Gentle break reminders so you never burn out

Perfect for:
— Studying for exams without doom-scrolling
— Remote work days that keep slipping away
— Anyone who opens their phone "for one second" and loses an hour

Download FocusFlow and reclaim your focus today.
```

**Google Play variant — full description reuses the above but weaves `pomodoro timer` and
`study timer` in twice more (feature bullets + a "Why FocusFlow" closing line) because Google
indexes the long description.**
