# S1-18 — App Store Listing Copy (ASO), done-for-you

**Positioning:** Indie app makers ship great apps and then write their App Store / Play Store
listing in five minutes at midnight. Weak title, no keywords, a description nobody reads →
downloads that never come. You rewrite the whole listing to rank and convert — per app, or on a
monthly retainer as they iterate.

**Target customer:** Solo iOS/Android devs and tiny app studios with a live app and <10k
downloads. They already spent months building; they'll happily pay $200–800 to stop leaving
installs on the table. Find them in r/iOSProgramming, r/androiddev, Indie Hackers, and the
"Show HN" / Product Hunt app launches from the last 90 days.

**Offer:** "I'll rewrite your App Store listing — title, subtitle, keyword field, and description —
optimized for the exact terms your buyers search. Ranking-ready copy in 48h. First listing audit
is free: send me your app link and I'll show you the 3 biggest ranking leaks."

**Price:** $200–800 per app · **$500/mo retainer** for ongoing keyword iteration + A/B copy
(most apps need 3–4 metadata rounds to find their winning terms — that's the recurring revenue).
