# Client Intake — 8 questions (no call needed)

Send this as a form or a plain message. Everything you need to write a listing without meeting.

1. **App store link(s)** (Apple and/or Google Play):
2. **In one sentence, what does your app do** for someone who's never heard of it?
3. **Who is it for?** (be specific — "students cramming for exams", not "everyone")
4. **What's the #1 outcome** a happy user gets?
5. **Which 3 apps do you consider competitors?** (links)
6. **Any words you MUST include or MUST avoid?** (trademarks, brand rules)
7. **Current traction** — downloads, rating, any press/awards I can cite as social proof?
8. **Which store matters most right now**, and is there a season/launch I should optimize for?

**Turnaround promise:** first draft within 48h of receiving this. One revision round included.

**Optional add-on to mention:** "I can also write your screenshot captions (the text overlays on
your store screenshots) — those convert harder than the description. +$150 or included in the
retainer." (This pairs naturally with S1-113 App-store screenshot design.)
