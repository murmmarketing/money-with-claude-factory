# Client Intake — 7 questions (no call needed)

1. **What does your product do?** One sentence, as if to a smart friend who's never seen it.
2. **Who is the video for?** The exact person watching — role, situation, what they're struggling with.
3. **What's the ONE thing** you want them to understand or feel by the end?
4. **What's the single action** at the end? (start free / book a demo / download / sign up)
5. **Target length?** (60s / 90s — I'll write to the second)
6. **Tone / brand references** — name 1–2 brands whose voice you like ("like Notion", "like a bank").
7. **Any must-say lines, claims to avoid, or proof points** (numbers, logos, results) I can use?

**Bonus (optional):** paste your homepage URL — I'll pull the pain and positioning straight from it.

**Turnaround:** first draft in 48h, two-column shoot-ready format, one revision round included.
I'll deliver an alternate hook line too, so you've got a cheap A/B choice.
