## 1. Value post — r/SaaS (also Indie Hackers "Growth" group)
**Title:** The reason your explainer video keeps stalling isn't the animation — it's the script

**Body:**
> I write explainer scripts and 90% of stalled video projects have the same problem: the script
> tries to explain everything, so it explains nothing. A 60s video is ~145 words. That's it.
> The fix — a 4-beat arc:
> 1. Open in the viewer's pain (not "Introducing…")
> 2. Show the ONE core mechanism, not every feature
> 3. Land on the outcome + one proof point
> 4. One CTA. Never two.
>
> I posted a full 75s sample script (two-column, voiceover + visual directions) in the comments.
> Happy to script one product free if you drop what it does 👇

(Post `scripts/saas-explainer.md` in the comments — it's your proof and your lead gen.)
