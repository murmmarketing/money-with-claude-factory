**Positioning:** SaaS founders and product marketers know they need a 60–90s explainer video, but
the script is where every project stalls — they can't compress "what we do" into 150 words that a
stranger gets in one watch. You write crisp, visually-directed scripts (problem → solution → proof →
CTA) that video editors can drop straight onto the timeline.

**Target customer:** Seed/early SaaS, app makers, and agencies/video editors who need scripts fast.
The editors are your best channel — they get script-less clients constantly and would rather refer
the writing than do it. Find founders in r/SaaS, Indie Hackers, and recent Product Hunt launches;
find editors on Upwork, Fiverr, and in motion-design communities.

**Offer:** "A 60–90 second explainer script your editor can shoot from tomorrow — problem, solution,
and CTA, timed to length with on-screen visual directions. 48-hour turnaround. First one: I'll
script your homepage hero video free so you can see the arc."

**Price:** $200–800 per script. Volume deal for editors/agencies (5 scripts/mo) — that's your
recurring revenue and warmest referral loop.
