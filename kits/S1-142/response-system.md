# Review-response system

**Speed:** reply within 24h. **Length:** 2–4 sentences. **Always:** thank by name, reference a
specific detail, invite back. **Never:** argue, over-apologize, or share private details publicly.

## 5-star
Thank + echo a specific thing they praised + warm invite back. Vary openings so it's not robotic.

## Mixed / 3-4 star
Thank for the honest feedback + acknowledge the gap specifically + one line on the fix + invite to
give another shot (offer to take it offline if useful).

## 1-2 star
Calm, human, no defensiveness. Acknowledge their experience isn't the standard + apologize once +
give a real contact to make it right ("please email {owner} at {email}"). Goal: show *future*
readers you handle problems well.

## Policy guardrails (client sets these)
- Never admit legal/medical fault. Route health/safety complaints to a private channel.
- Approved comp/refund language only. No pricing promises in public.
