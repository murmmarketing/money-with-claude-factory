# S1-142 — Review-Response Automation (done-for-you)

**Positioning:** Local businesses (restaurants, clinics, gyms, hotels) get Google/Yelp reviews
they never reply to — and unanswered reviews cost them ranking and trust. You reply to every
review in their brand voice, on policy, monthly, per location.

**Target customer:** Multi-location local businesses and the agencies that serve them. Owners
who already "know" reviews matter but have no time. Find them by literally reading Google Maps:
businesses with 50+ reviews and few owner responses = your prospect list.

**Offer:** "Every review gets an on-brand reply within 24h — handled for you. $99/mo per location.
First month: I'll reply to your last 20 reviews free so you can see the voice."

**Price:** $50–200/mo per location. Bundle 5+ locations at a discount for agencies (they resell).

## Launch in 60 minutes
1. Read `response-system.md` — the reply framework (5-star / mixed / 1-star / policy lines).
2. Read `voice-intake.md` — the 6 questions that capture a client's brand voice.
3. Pick ONE real local business, draft replies to their 5 most recent unanswered reviews using
   `example-replies.md` as the model, and send it as a free sample (marketing.md).
4. **[YOUR MOVE]** connect billing + agree how they'll approve/post replies (see YOUR-MOVE.md).

## Why it's sticky
It's recurring by nature (reviews arrive weekly), low-touch once voice is set, and the client
never wants to take it back. 20 locations × $99 = ~$2k/mo of boring, durable revenue.
