# Brand-voice intake (send to each new client)
1. In 3 words, how should your business sound? (e.g. warm, expert, playful)
2. Do you address reviewers by first name? Formal or casual?
3. One phrase you love / one you'd never say?
4. Who signs the replies? (owner name / "the team")
5. For a bad review, what can you offer to make it right? (contact, comp, refund policy)
6. Any topics to route offline (health, legal, staff complaints)?

Feed answers to Claude with response-system.md to generate replies on-brand.
